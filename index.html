ABOUT GALLERY CONTACT
If you would like to get in touch with me, or if you are interested in purchasing prints, please contact me via email at scott.lucchini@gmail.com.
I've been interested in photography off and on throughout my whole life. My parents bought a DSLR when I was in middle school and I couldn't get enough. I demanded to be the photographer whenever we went on family vacations and my parents were always disappointed since I would never take any photos with people in them. I just loved photographing the landscape and the environment.
In 2016, my wife and I moved to Edinburgh, Scotland for a year and that's when my photography really took off again. There are so many beautiful things in that country, I could hardly resist. Not only did I love documenting our tourism around Scotland, England, and Europe, but just wandering around Edinburgh, there was so much to work with. During this time, I bought my Olympus OMD E-M10 Mk II mirrorless camera that I've been using ever since. Not only does it produce fantastic photos, it is the perfect size to take along with me everywhere I go so as to be sure that I never miss a shot.
We now live in Madison, Wisconsin where I am pursuing a PhD in theoretical physics. In addition to photography, my hobbies include swing dancing, programming, hiking, and various motorsports.
